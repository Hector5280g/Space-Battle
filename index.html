** Moves & Instructions Are Below **

To attack first ship with lasers use/type hero.laser(Alien1)

To attack first ship with missles use/type hero.attack(Alien1)

To laser or missle the second ship replace al1 above with Alien2

It is the same with subsequent enemies... Alien3 ... Alien4 ... Alien5 ... Alien6 ... Alien 7

hero.laser(Alien2) ... hero.laser(Alien3 ) or hero.attack(Alien4) ... hero.attack(Alien2) and so on ...

To check your hull strength (health) use/type hero.announceHealth()
